Problem & Solution

Our problem

Middle school is where the most frequent and severe bullying occurs today. Our project addresses the need for teachers and administrators to track and gain insight into bullying behavior trends in order to implement or refine current school policies to address bullying among middle school students. Our web-based design offers tools to teachers that expand solutions beyond simply punishing individual students and promote systemic, school wide changes and track their results.
